Giraffes are awesome!

The giraffe is a large African hoofed mammal belonging to the genus Giraffa. It is the tallest living terrestrial animal and the largest ruminant on Earth.

Some random giraffe facts

Giraffes’ tongues can be up to 20 inches long
A group of giraffes can be identified as a journey or tower
Giraffes walk by moving both legs on the same side of their body together
Giraffes’ closest living relative is the okapi
